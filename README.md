 **Project Overview**

This project presents a comprehensive Sales Analysis Dashboard developed using Power BI.
The dashboard provides key insights into sales performance across product categories, regions, customer segments, and individual orders.

The objective of this project is to analyze business sales data and derive actionable insights for decision-making.

**Tools & Technologies Used**

Power BI

Data Modeling

DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

Data Cleaning & Transformation

Data Visualization

**Dataset Information**

The dataset contains:

Order ID

Product Name

Sub-Category

Category

Segment

Region

Sales Amount

Customer Information

**Dashboard Features**


1️⃣ Sales by Sub-Category

Identifies top-performing product sub-categories

Helps in inventory and marketing decisions

2️⃣ Sales by Product Name

Displays top-selling products

Useful for product-level performance analysis

3️⃣ Sales by Segment

Analyzes sales contribution from:

Consumer

Corporate

Home Office

4️⃣ Sales by Region

Compares sales performance across regions:

West

East

Central

South

5️⃣ Sales by Order ID

Shows distribution of sales across different orders

Helps identify high-value transactions


**Key Insights**

Consumer segment contributes the highest share of total sales.

West region generates the highest revenue.

Phones and Chairs are top-performing sub-categories.

Certain products dominate overall sales contribution.


**Business Impact**

This dashboard helps:

Identify high-performing products

Compare regional sales performance

Understand customer segment contribution

Make data-driven business decisions
